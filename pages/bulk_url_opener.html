Bulk URL Opener

Show Description
Opens a list of domains or URLs that you input. Helpful for analyzing batches of URLs.

Instructions:
  1. Paste your list of URLs (one per line) into the text area below.
  2. Set the number of URLs to open at a time (to avoid overwhelming your browser).
  3. Click "Open Next Batch" to open the first batch of URLs in new tabs. Repeat as needed to open the next batches.
You must allow pop-ups for this tool to function properly!
